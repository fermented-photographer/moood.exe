ABOUT
KRUPA

"I picked up my first camera two years ago and haven't put it down since. Every shoot teaches me something new — that's what keeps me going."

Based in Bengaluru, I'm a self-taught photographer finding my voice through portraits, street life, and whatever light the day offers. Still early in the journey — but deeply committed to it.

I shoot mostly on a single camera and natural light. No fancy studio — just curiosity, patience, and a good eye for the moment.
